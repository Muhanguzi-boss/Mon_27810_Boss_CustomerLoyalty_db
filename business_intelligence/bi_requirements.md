# Business Intelligence Requirements

This document outlines the BI requirements for the Loyalty System project.

---

## Objectives
- Provide managers with insights into customer behavior, reward redemption, and purchase trends.
- Enable tracking of loyalty program effectiveness.
- Support decision-making with clear KPIs and dashboards.

---

## Functional Requirements
1. **Customer Insights**
   - View top customers by points balance.
   - Track points earned vs points redeemed.

2. **Reward Analytics**
   - Identify most redeemed rewards.
   - Monitor points consumption trends.

3. **Purchase Analytics**
   - Monthly/weekly sales trends.
   - Weekend vs weekday purchase comparison.

4. **Audit & Compliance**
   - Track denied vs allowed operations.
   - Monitor employee activity on restricted days.

---

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Queries must run efficiently on large datasets.
- Dashboards must be clear and intuitive.
- Audit logs must be tamper-proof and accessible only to authorized users.
