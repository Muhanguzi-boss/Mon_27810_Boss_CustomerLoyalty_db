# Dashboards

This document describes the dashboards designed for the Loyalty System.

---

## Customer Dashboard
- **Top Customers by Points** (bar chart).
- **Points Earned vs Redeemed** (stacked bar chart).
- **Customer Growth Over Time** (line chart).

## Rewards Dashboard
- **Most Redeemed Rewards** (pie chart).
- **Total Points Redeemed per Reward** (bar chart).
- **Reward Redemption Trends** (line chart by month).

## Purchases Dashboard
- **Monthly Sales Trend** (line chart).
- **Weekend vs Weekday Purchases** (column chart).
- **Average Purchase Value per Customer** (scatter plot).

## Audit Dashboard
- **Allowed vs Denied Operations** (donut chart).
- **Operations by Action Type (INSERT/UPDATE/DELETE)** (bar chart).
- **Audit Log Timeline** (line chart showing attempts over time).
